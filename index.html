About

Sir Robert Bryson Hall II, known professionally as Logic, is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, record producer and author.

Logic released his debut studio album Under Pressure in 2014 which peaked at number four on the U.S. top 200. This was his first album to hit gold.

In 2017 his career hit a breakthrough with the release of his third studio album Everybody. The album went platinum and was the first time Logic hit the number one slot on Billboard 200. This album contained the popular song "1-800-273-8255", a top-ten hit internationally.

Logic had many successful albums since his first to go platinum including Bobby Tarantino II, YSIV, and Confessions of a Dangerous Mind. He has done many major colloborations with shows like Rick and Morty and rappers like Joyner Lucas and Eminem.

Other than music, Logic released a book named Supermarket which released alongside the soundtrack of the same name. This book went on to become a New York Times Best Seller.
